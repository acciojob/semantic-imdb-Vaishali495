Dhurandhar

Brief Summary of the plot

Directed by Aditya Dhar, Dhurandhar is an action-thriller following Jaskirat Singh, an Indian army aspirant who—after a devastating personal tragedy—is recruited by RAW handler Ajay Sanyal for a covert mission. He goes undercover in Pakistan's underworld as "Hamza" to infiltrate criminal syndicates and dismantle networks targeting India.

The main cast of actors and their roles include:

  • Ranveer Singh as Hamza Ali Mazari
  • Akshaye Khanna as the antagonist Rehman Dakait
  • R. Madhavan as Ajay Sanyal
  • Sanjay Dutt as S.P. Choudhary Aslam
  • Arjun Rampalas Major Iqbal
  • Sara Arjun as Yalina Jamali
  • Danish Pandor as Uzair Baloch
